Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy is provided by Brandon Brown to inform you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for the mobile application Mise En Label.

1. Permissions and Data Access

To provide its core functionality, Mise En Label requires access to certain sensitive device features. We process this data ephemerally (locally on your device) and do not store it on our personal servers:

  • Bluetooth & USB: Used to communicate with external hardware devices.
  • Camera & Microphone: Used for in-app features requiring audio/visual input.
  • Files and Storage: Used to allow you to select, upload, and manage your own data within the app.
  • Contacts: Accessed locally to facilitate user-led interactions.

2. Third-Party Services (Login & Payments)

We use trusted third-party services to manage secure parts of the app experience. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Clerk: Used for secure user authentication and account management.
  • Google Play Billing: Used to process subscriptions and commercial transactions.

3. Data Collection (Section 11 Compliance)

Because Mise En Label offers a subscription service and user accounts via Clerk, we (through our service providers) process Identifiers (such as email/name) and Commercial Information (subscription status). This data is used solely to provide the services you have purchased.

4. Account and Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and associated data at any time. You can initiate this within the app settings or by contacting us at the email address below. Upon request, we will ensure your account is removed from our authentication provider (Clerk).
